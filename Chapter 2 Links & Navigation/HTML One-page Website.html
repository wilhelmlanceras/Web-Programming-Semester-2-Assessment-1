Haikyuu!!

It is an anime about high school volleyball. The series highlight Shoyo Hinata. Alongside his team, including the ace player Kageyama, they aim for greatness in volleyball tournaments.

Cast and Creative Team

  • Ayumu Murase as Shoyo Hinata
  • Kaito Ishikawa as Tobio Kageyama
  • Nobuhiko Okamoto as Yamaguchi Tadashi
  • Yoshimasa Hosoya as Asahi Azumane
  • Miyu Irino as Kageyama Tobio
  • Koichi Koyama as Daichi Sawamura

Director: Susumu Mitsunaka

Studio: Production I.G

Key Characters

  • Shoyo Hinata: The passionate and energetic protagonist who dreams of becoming a great volleyball player despite his height disadvantage.
  • Tobio Kageyama: A genius setter with incredible skills but a perfectionist attitude. He and Hinata form a powerful duo.
  • Daichi Sawamura: The calm and reliable captain of Karasuno's volleyball team.
  • Asahi Azumane: The strong and dependable ace of the team, who faces personal doubts but always steps up for his team.
  • Yamaguchi Tadashi: A shy but steadily improving player who helps the team when needed.
